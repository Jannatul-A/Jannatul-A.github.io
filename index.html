Sports Clothing Retail Revenue Analysis

Analysed product, pricing, review, and revenue data for an online sports clothing retailer to generate actionable recommendations for marketing and sales teams.

  • Adidas "Elite" products generated the highest revenue (~$4.6M) across all price tiers
  • Nike offers zero discounts vs Adidas's ~33% — raising key pricing strategy questions
  • Reviews strongly correlate with revenue (r = 0.65); Q1 drives peak review volume

International Debt Statistics Analysis

Explored World Bank international debt data across 124 developing countries and 25 debt indicators to uncover patterns in global economic debt.

  • Total developing world debt exceeds $3 trillion USD across all categories
  • China carries the highest debt load at ~$285.8B — nearly double the next country
  • Long-term debt repayment averages ~$5.9B per country and is universal across all 124 nations
